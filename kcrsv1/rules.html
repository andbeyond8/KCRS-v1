Rules to be followed

It is for the safety of all the players

1. Waiver must be signed and fees paid before a person can play.

2. Except where noted on this form all FIFA rules of soccer shall apply.

3. Half field may be used for a smaller amount of participants

4. No unsportsmanlike conduct

5. No smoking on the pitch, parking or spectator areas (including e-cigarettes)

6. No alcohol on the pitch, parking or spectator areas

7. No profanity or offensive language

8. No harassment of any kind

9. No fighting

10. No weapons of any kind

Expectations of Players

- This is a non-competitive league and there is NO tolerance for aggressive play

- Adjust your level of play to accommodate slower and less skilled players

- Give everyone an opportunity to play the ball

- Larger and faster players should take extra care on the field

- No slide or tackles from behind and don't go in hard for a 50-50 ball challege.

- Bring both a dark and white shirt to each game

Organizers retain the right to exclude players who do not abide by the rules and expectations of this league and to exclude players who are suspected of any sort of abuse or harassment.
